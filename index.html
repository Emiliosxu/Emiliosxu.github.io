About Me

My name is Emilio Jaramillo and I am a Computer Science major graduating in May of 2026. A little more about me I'm a Chicago native with a strong background in music. I started playing guitar at the age of eight and eventually performed in a local Mexican regional band.This experience has shaped my creativity and also a curiosity for music gear and recording technology. In high school, discovering computer science allowed me to blend my passion for music with programming and product development. My experience as both a musician and a consumer of music products has given me valuable insights into user needs, which I aim to apply throughout my career in tech. Learning how to program has given me the ability to understand the digital tools I use in music. This has fueled a passion for computer science and has inspired me to develop tools for the world to use.
